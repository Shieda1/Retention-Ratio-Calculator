What is the retention ratio calculator?
The retention rate calculator is a tool that determines the number of customers that stay with a business after a period of time.
How to calculate retention ratio?
To calculate retention ratio you should use the following formula:
retention ratio = ((net income - dividends paid) / net income) * 100
Why use retention ratio calculator?
The retention rate calculator is used in order to calculate the number of customers that stay with a business after a period of time.
